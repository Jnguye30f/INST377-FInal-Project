What is the problem?

with the internet and softwares growing more complex, the number of security vulnerabilities also increases. Many small businesses and individuals lack access to real time Cybersecurity and threat intelligence, and data is often buried deep in databases or expensive pay walls.

Solution

With this web apllication it provides a simple interface to search recent Cybersecurity vulnerabilities from the National Vulnerability Database (NVD). By utilizing the search feature to view key details, find keywords, and save important vulnerabilities to your personal watchlists.
